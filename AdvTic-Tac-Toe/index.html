HOW TO PLAY

01
The board is a 9×9 grid — nine 3×3 mini Tic-Tac-Toe boards arranged in a 3×3 meta-grid.
02
Player X always goes first and can click any empty cell on the first move.
03
Key Rule: The position of the cell you play inside its mini-board determines which mini-board your opponent must play in next. E.g. if you play in position 5 (center) of any mini-board, the next player must play somewhere in mini-board #5.
04
If the forced mini-board is fully filled, the active player may play in any mini-board that still has empty cells.
05
Win a mini-board by placing three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) inside it. The board is then marked with your symbol.
06
Once a mini-board is claimed, it retains the winner's symbol — even if the opponent later fills it. The result does not change.
07
Players can still place moves in a won mini-board as long as empty cells remain, but it won't change the board's ownership.
08
Win the game by claiming three mini-boards in a row on the meta-grid. The meta-board follows standard Tic-Tac-Toe winning lines.
09
🟡 Gold highlight = the mini-board where the next player MUST play. Use the unhighlighted boards to plan your strategy!
10
Use Undo ↩ and Redo ↪ in the toolbar to travel through move history freely.
